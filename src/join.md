Our club hosts weekly online practice [contests](https://vjudge.net/group/uw-cp-club). Everyone is encouraged to give them a shot! Our [Discord](https://discord.gg/KkyKMrfs) has instructions on how to participate.

Every week we meet in-person to discuss solutions to these contests. Check our [Discord](https://discord.gg/KkyKMrfs) for meeting time and location. Everyone is welcome to come!
